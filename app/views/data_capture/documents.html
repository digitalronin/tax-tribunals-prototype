For your case to proceed without delay you need to make sure the correct documents are sent. We can accept scanned images or good quality pictures, for example via a smartphone. If you are unable to upload your documents you will need to post them to us within 14 days of your application being submitted.

Documents to upload:

  • A copy of the HMRC decision(s) I am appealing against
  • A copy of any statement of reasons for that decision(s)
  • A copy of any notice of conclusions of an HMRC Review
  • A copy of my appeal to HMRC (Direct tax only)
  • A copy of any hardship decision by HMRC or any letter to HMRC regarding hardship (Indirect tax only)
