Appeal to an independent tax tribunal

Use this service to challenge decisions by HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC).

The tribunal is independent of government and will listen to both sides of the argument before making a decision.

For more information see the guide to tax tribunals and fees.

You can make an appeal to the tax tribunal by completing each of the steps below.

  1. Find out your fee: £20, £50 or £200 (about 5 minutes to complete)
  2. Check you're in time (5 minutes)
  3. Enter appeal details (20 minutes)
  4. Pay fee (5 minutes)

Other ways to submit an appeal

  • You can submit an appeal or application by paper form
  • Send your form by post to First-tier Tribunal (Tax Chamber), PO Box 16972, Birmingham, B16 6TZ
