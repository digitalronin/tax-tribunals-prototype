1. Find out your fee

Before your appeal can start, you will need to pay an administration fee so the tribunal can process your case.

For certain types of appeal, you will also have to pay further fees depending on whether your case needs needs further documents, correspondence and a hearing in person with a judge. The amount depends on your type of case.

When the judge makes a decision on your case, they also have the power to decide whether HMRC should reimburse your tax tribunal fees. The tribunal itself does not have the power to enforce or award costs.

Check if you’re able to get help paying your feeif you’re on certain benefits or a low income.
