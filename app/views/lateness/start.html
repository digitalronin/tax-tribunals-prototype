Check you meet the tribunal deadline

You must appeal within the time stated on your last letter from HMRC. This is usually 30 days.

If you have asked HMRC for a review with a tax officer not originally involved in your case, you should wait until the review finishes. When you receive your letter from HMRC with the review conclusions you are then able to make an appeal to the tax tribunal.

What you will need

  • date you received your last letter from HMRC
  • reasons why you're outside the time limit if you want to submit an appeal late
