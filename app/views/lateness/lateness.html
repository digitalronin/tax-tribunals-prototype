Do you think you are late to appeal to the Tax Tribunal?

You have up to 30 days to appeal a HMRC decision.

Use the date from the last letter you received. When a review is completed by HMRC you should receive what's called a 'decision letter'. If you've not received a decision letter, use the last letter you've received from HMRC.

Help with types of appeal

You can only choose one reason per appeal. For example if you wanted to appeal against both a penalty and the amount you owe, you will need to make two separate appeals to the tax tribunal.
